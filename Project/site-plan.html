FitTrack Gym Planner

Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

FitTrack Gym Planner

This name represents a fitness-focused website designed to help users explore gym workouts and plan their training routines. “FitTrack” emphasizes progress and consistency, while “Gym Planner” clearly describes the purpose of the site.

Optional domain availability: fittrackgym.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide structured and accessible information about gym workouts and exercise routines. The site will allow users to browse exercises by muscle group, learn basic training tips, and interact with dynamic features such as saving favorite workouts.

Scenarios

  • What exercises can I do to train a specific muscle group such as chest or legs?
  • How can I save my favorite workouts to review them later?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1E1E1E – headers, navigation, footer.
  • Secondary Color: #2ECC71 – accents, highlights, borders.
  • Background Color: #F4F4F4 – page background.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
